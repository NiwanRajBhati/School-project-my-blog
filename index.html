JIM Corbett Nation Park

[]
i had visited in june 2022

Jim Corbett National Park is in Uttarakhand. It is the oldest National Park in India. It was the first park under the Project Tiger initiative. There are 110 tree species, 50 mammal species, 580 bird species and 25 reptile species. In 1935, this stretch of Ramganga was protected to protect wild animals. In 1957, the park was named ‘Jim Corbett National Park’ It is located near Ramnagar town in the Nainital district of Uttarakhand. Currently, more than 70,000 visitors visit the park every season. The area of this park is 1318.54 square kilometres. It is also an eco-tourism destination and is home to 488 species of plants and animal diversity.

About My School

MPS International
Best School In Jaipur

MPS International, a Co-educational CBSE Affiliated English Medium School, located at Bhabha Marg, Tilak Nagar, Jaipur, founded in 2014 with affiliation no: 1730679, is modern and attractive in both layout and construction.
