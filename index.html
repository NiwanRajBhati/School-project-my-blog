Ranthambore Safari Park

i had visited in june 2022

Ranthambore National Park is a famous wildlife sanctuary in India. It is located in the state of Rajasthan and is known for its tigers. The park is named after the historic Ranthambore Fort, which is situated within its boundaries. Ranthambore is a home to many animals like deer, monkeys, and various bird species. The park has lush green forests and rocky hills, providing a perfect habitat for wildlife. It is a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers who come to see the majestic tigers. Ranthambore offers exciting jeep safaris to explore the park and spot animals in their natural habitat. Park rangers and forest officials protect the animals and ensure their safety within the park. Ranthambore National Park plays a significant role in tiger conservation and preserving the biodiversity of the region. As Class 1 students, we can learn about the importance of protecting wildlife and their habitats from places like Ranthambore National Park.
